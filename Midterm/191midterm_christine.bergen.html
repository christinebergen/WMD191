Descriptions

Zen Rabbits

Zen Rabbits are the calmest of all the VIU rabbits. You will often find them basking in the sunshine in the Japanese garden. They love green tea and will likely approach you and ask for a sip if you walk by with a mug of it.

Gym Rabbits

Gym rabbits hang out by the Gym (building 190). They spend much of their time running up and down the stairs in front of the library in search of the elusive carrot on a string.

The Wilds

Often found in the empty lot near 4th street, the wilds spend much of their time at the campus pub. The wilds are usually not spotted until late morning or early afternoon. They spent most of their time partying and not paying attention in class, and often sleep until noon.
